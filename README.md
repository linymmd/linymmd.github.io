# About me
I am a **Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology** Fellow at the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center. See my **[Curriculum Vitae](https://linymmd.github.io/CV_YMLin.pdf)**. I actively participate in various clinical research projects, focusing on the application of innovative diagnostic imaging tools and minimally invasive image-guided treatments for cancer patients. 

I earned my M.D. from National Yang-Ming University in Taiwan in 2013, followed by a four-year Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology residency and a Body Imaging fellowship at Taipei Veterans General Hospital (2015–2020). From 2020 to 2023, I conducted postdoctoral research at the University of Texas MD Anderson Cancer Center, focusing on minimally invasive, image-guided cancer treatments under the mentorship of Dr. Bruno Odisio.

I love music, cycling, jogging, and travel.
